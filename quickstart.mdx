## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before you begin">
Ensure you have:
- Access to the Hello dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com`
- A registered account with your email verified
- Basic knowledge of your business vertical (EdTech, Booking, or Corporate/AI)
</Callout>

## Access the ROI Wizard

The ROI Wizard helps you generate a customized business case based on your vertical. It calculates potential revenue gains, cost savings, and implementation timelines.

Visit `https://dashboard.example.com/roi-wizard` to start.

## Generate Your Business Case

Follow these steps to create your first project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Run ROI Wizard" icon="zap">
    Enter your metrics:
    
    - Current monthly revenue
    - Admin headcount
    - No-show rate (for booking)
    
    Click "Generate Report" to get your personalized ROI projection.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review the Report" icon="bar-chart">
    The wizard outputs:
    
    | Metric              | Projected Improvement |
    |---------------------|-----------------------|
    | Revenue Growth      | `+25%` in 3 months   |
    | Admin Time Saved    | `40 hours/week`      |
    | ROI Timeline        | `6 months` payback   |
    
    Download the PDF for your team.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Initiate Contact" icon="mail">
    Use the "Contact Team" button in the report.
    
    Provide:
    - Your generated report ID
    - Preferred vertical
    - Timeline for kickoff
    
    Expect a response within `24 hours`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configure Your First Project

Set up initial project settings via the dashboard API.

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,cURL">
  ```javascript
  const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/projects', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: {
      'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY',
      'Content-Type': 'application/json'
    },
    body: JSON.stringify({
      name: 'EdTech Onboarding Automation',
      vertical: 'edtech'
    })
  });
  
  const project = await response.json();
  console.log(`Project ID: ${project.id}`);
  ```
  
  ```bash
  curl -X POST https://api.example.com/v1/projects \
    -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
    -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
    -d '{
      "name": "EdTech Onboarding Automation",
      "vertical": "edtech"
    }'
  ```
</CodeGroup>

<ParamField path="vertical" param-type="string" required="true">
  Your business vertical: `edtech`, `booking`, or `corporate`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField header="Authorization" param-type="string" required="true">
  Bearer token from dashboard settings.
</ParamField>

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting Common Issues" default-open="false">
- **Wizard not loading?** Clear browser cache and try incognito mode.
- **API errors?** Verify your `{API_KEY}` is active in dashboard > Settings.
- **Contact no response?** Check spam folder for `noreply@hello.com`.
