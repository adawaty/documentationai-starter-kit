## Overview

Upload a CSV dataset to Hello for training your ML model.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare your CSV">
    Make sure your dataset is in CSV format, headers included.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload via API">
    ````bash
    curl -X POST https://hello.minasaat.com/v1/datasets \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN" \
      -F "file=@/path/to/your/data.csv"
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check status">
    View your dataset at `https://dashboard.minasaat.com/datasets`
  </Step>
</Steps>
